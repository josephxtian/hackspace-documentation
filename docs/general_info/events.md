# Events
Events can be run throughout the year when we have volunteers 

They are posted first and foremost to the [forum]({{ links.forum }}/c/events/12), and our monthly member newsletter often provides a summary.

## Members Meetings
*~3-6 times a year, usually a weeknight, ~90 minutes*

A chance to discuss the goings on in the space, updates from the areas, and bigger topics of discussion. Any voting matters can also be held at these meetings. Notice of a meeting will be posted to the [forum]({{ links.forum }}c/events/meetings/35) in advance.

Any member can call a members meeting if they have something to discuss.

!!! info
    If you cannot attend a meeting, please arrange for a proxy to represent you at the meeting. Votes can only be held if a proportion of the membership is represented at the meeting.

## Hack the Space Days
*3-4 times a year. Usually a saturday and/or sunday*

Members come together to improve the space, eat pizza and meet other makers. A list of tasks is posted on the [forum]({{ links.forum }}c/events/hack-the-space/10) in advance, and members are encouraged to take ownership of the jobs in advance to make the most of the day.

Before a Hack the Space Day is confirmed, a call for members to volunteer to coordinate the day is put out.

## Socials
We have two main socials each year:
1. Christmas Party - Last Wednesday evening before christmas.
2. Summer Party - BBQ-type activity sometime in summer.

Members are welcome to organise further socials through the year. See below for information about organising events.
