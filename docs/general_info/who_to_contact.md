# Who to Contact

The Hackspace is member run community, so for most things you'll want to speak to regular members via the [forum]({{ links.forum }}) or [telegram]({{ links.telegram }}). See our [communication page](../getting_started/communication.md) for more information.

If you want to discuss something that is private in nature please see the below options.

## Online Forms

We have a couple of online forms which are directed to the best placed volunteers:

### Health & Saftey
E.g. Report a near miss, accident, broken tools, etc. please [fill in this form]({{ links.accident_form }}) which will be forwarded to the relevant team. This is the QR code link shown around the space.

### Member Incident
E.g. Issues with other members, rule breaking, inappropriate behaviour .Please [fill in this form]({{ links.formal_incident_form }}) which can only be seen by board members. If you wish to make a complaint about a member of the Board, you can find individual board member's contact information on the notice board in the space.

## Board

Some issues may require contacting the board of directors, <board@hacman.org.uk>. This could include problems with payment processing or the members portal, as well as critical issues and [emergencies](./emergency_procedure.md). Please note,the board are volunteers and it may take up to 2 weeks to recieve a response.

## General Enquiries

For all other enquiries contact <info@hacman.org.uk>, this may include:

-	Community outreach
-	Running an event
-	Members storage issues
