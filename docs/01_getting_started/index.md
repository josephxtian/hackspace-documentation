# Getting Started

Welcome to Manchester Hackspace!

There's lots to know about our community, but we've provided a run-down of the essentials in this section. As you get more familiar with the space, we encourage you to keep referring back to this documentation to learn more about our processes.

Before you can complete the steps here, please [visit us](https://www.hacman.org.uk/visit-us/) for a tour, to check your aims align with ours

Set up an account on our [membership portal](https://members.hacman.org.uk/) and enter the password you were given on your tour. You can then set up a direct debit, which will activate your account.
